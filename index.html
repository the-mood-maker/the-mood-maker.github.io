Process

How it works?

The UMD MoodMaker App allows students to track and adjust their style of living. It will communicate with the user to provide suggestions and techniques to live a more active, social and happier lifestyle.

Sign Up

Create a free account from scratch or via Google. Either way, a personal account gives you the ability to personalize and control your experience with the Mood Maker.

Chat

Want to talk about or take note of how you're feeling? The Mood Maker Chatbulb and Mini-Diaries are quick, readily available and here to lend a listening ear.

Act

Once you've expressed your current situation of emotions, take your mind off of things with a carefully-suggested musical, fitness or food-related activity.

Track

Keep track of your daily chat sessions, diary entries and activities with the Mood Log.

Features

Great features you'll love.

The Mood Maker comes equipped with a small yet carefully selected variety of features you can use to track, discuss and initiate your own personal solutions to improve upon your mood in real-time.

Personalization

With the ability to make and customize a personal profile, users can feel more at home with The Mood Maker and less like another number performing for an algorithm.

24/7 Chatbulb

The Chatbulb is where users speak out on their present thoughts and feelings. Whether you're feeling great or not-so-great, breif sessions with the chatbulb allow you to track and acknowledge the when and why of those emotions.

Mini-Diaries

In addition to a brief check-in session with the chatbulb, users can also go further into the details of their emotions or anxiety by writing small blurbs with the Mini-Diaries.

The Mood Log

This automated log is how users can keep track of all their sessions, diary entries and activities they've engaged in over time. This offers them the ability to look over and observe their progress.
